This infographic highlights digital literacy awareness, explaining that it is the ability to use digital devices, apps, and the internet 
effectively, safely, and responsibly. It showcases useful tools for students like Google Docs, Notion, Coursera, and Google Calendar to improve 
productivity and learning. It also emphasizes safe internet practices such as creating strong passwords, protecting personal information, and 
avoiding suspicious links or phishing attempts.
